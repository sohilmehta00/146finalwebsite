Moran's

Price Range: $$

Address: 501 Gardent St.

Description: Established in 1989, Moran's was originally a pub until they introduced their food menu in January of 2016. They offer classic bar food such as Buffalo Wings and Burgers along with unique entrees such as an Irish breakfast and "Lamb Lollipops". Moran's is closed on Monday, opened from 5PM-2AM on Tuesday-Thursday, 11;30AM-3AM on Friday, 11AM-3AM on Saturday, and 11AM-2AM on Sunday.
